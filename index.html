Prediction & Risk Identification for Student Mentorship

AI-powered early warning system to reduce student dropouts

Rule-based Risk

Admin configures thresholds and the system automatically applies clear rules to categorize students into High, Medium, or Low risk with color-coding for clarity.

Mentor View

Mentors get visual cards, student trends, and quick-notify controls enabling them to identify issues early and act fast to support their students.

Interactive Data Insights

Dive into student data with dynamic and interactive charts. Track attendance trends, monitor performance, analyze fee status, and spot at-risk students—all in one visual dashboard.

Early Warning Alerts

Receive instant alerts for students with falling attendance or grades. Identify risks early and act quickly. Support mentors in preventing dropouts.
